## 🔗 File Structure Reference

### Where to Find Things

**Chatbot Code**
- Endpoint: `app/routes/chatbot.py`
- Widget: `templates/base.html` (in `<style>` and `<script>`)
- Documentation: `CHATBOT_FEATURE.md`

**Dashboard UI**
- Template: `templates/admin/dashboard.html`
- Styling: `templates/admin/dashboard.html` (in `<style>`)
- Documentation: `DASHBOARD_UI_REDESIGN.md`

**Navigation & Layout**
- Master Template: `templates/base.html`
- Styling: `templates/base.html` + `static/css/style.css`
- Documentation: `INTEGRATION_GUIDE.md`

**Configuration**
- App Factory: `app/__init__.py`
- Routes: `app/routes/chatbot.py`
- Documentation: `INTEGRATION_GUIDE.md - Configuration`
